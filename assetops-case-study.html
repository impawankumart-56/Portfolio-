Case Study · Asset Management

AssetOps
Scalable Asset Lifecycle Design

Designing scalable asset management and approval workflows, from request to retirement, with complete visibility and control.

PlatformMobile & Web
ScopeEnterprise
RoleUI/UX Designer
Duration4 Months
Impact75% Faster Approvals
Actual design screens are protected under NDA. Only conceptual flows and process diagrams are shown here.
Problem

Organizations managing IT and sales assets rely on manual tracking, disconnected systems, and unclear approval chains — creating systematic inefficiency across every operational stage.

Inefficient asset allocationNo centralized inventoryNo real-time visibilityManual billing errorsNo audit trailComplex rollover logicUnstructured scrap management
6 Key Modules
01
Asset Requesting
Raise asset requests with pre-filled categories and pricing. Simplified creation with minimal manual effort.
02
Multi-Level Approvals
Real-time tracking of request status. Comment-based approval system with full visibility across all stages.
03
Billing Automation
Auto-calculation of total, paid, and due amounts. Eliminated manual billing errors entirely.
04
Asset Rollover
Reassign assets between users with complete history. Quick search and filter-based reassignment.
05
Scrap Management
Structured scrap request flow with one-step approval. Clear tracking of all discarded assets.
06
Mobile & Desktop Access
Fully responsive. Same workflows across devices — optimised for on-the-go actions without losing functionality.
Design Decisions
Modular Workflow Architecture
Clear modules: Request → Approval → Billing → Assignment → Rollover → Scrap. Improved both clarity and future scalability from day one.
Real-Time Approval Visibility
Status tracking with inline comments. Shows who approved, what comments were added, and current stage — improving transparency and accountability throughout the chain.
Simplified Request Creation
Pre-filled fields (categories, pricing), reduced manual entry, and guided form structure. Made asset requests faster and error-free from the first interaction.
Automated Billing Experience
UI surfaces total cost, paid vs due amounts automatically — removing manual calculations and eliminating an entire category of financial errors from operations.
Asset Lifecycle Visibility
Clear tracking for assigned, reassigned, and scrapped assets — full visibility from the moment an asset is requested to when it's retired, with complete audit trail.
Impact

Reflection

Good enterprise design is not just about usability — it's about visibility, control, and trust.

This project showed how to design for complex, multi-stakeholder enterprise systems. Reduced manual errors in allocation and billing. Improved cross-department coordination. Enabled real-time tracking and faster decisions. Created complete audit trails for compliance.
